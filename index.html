Our Heritage & Craft

Founded in the heart of Switzerland, our brand has stood for precision for over a century. We believe that a watch is more than just a tool; it is a statement of identity and a legacy to be passed down.

Every gear and spring is hand-assembled by master watchmakers who have dedicated their lives to the craft of timekeeping. The process for a single timepiece can take over a year of meticulous labor.
